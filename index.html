Keyboards

I’m passionate about building custom mechanical keyboards. For me, they’re more than just a tool—they're how we connect with others, support our work, and express our creativity. They become an extension of ourselves. The three things that I focus on for keyboards are as follow:

  • Design – Personalization through form, function, and tactile creativity.
  • Sound – Rich sound that enhances the feel of each keystroke.
  • Feel – Tailored tactility that connects touch with comfort and precision.
