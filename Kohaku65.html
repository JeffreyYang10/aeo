About the Kohaku65

The Kohaku65, designed by SingaKBD, is widely regarded as an "endgame" or "grail" keyboard among enthusiasts. Inspired by the elegance and symbolism of koi fish, the design reflects serenity and beauty, with intricate details that evoke the graceful movement of koi in water. This high-end keyboard is renowned for its seamless construction, premium materials, and perfectly tuned acoustics. The incorporation of koi fish elements not only adds to its aesthetic appeal but also imbues the Kohaku65 with a deeper sense of artistry. For many, this masterpiece represents the pinnacle of custom keyboard craftsmanship, making it the crown jewel of their collection.
