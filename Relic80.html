About the Relic80

The Relic80 is an 80% mechanical keyboard that blends modern technology with an artistic, timeworn aesthetic. Inspired by the works of Daniel Arsham, the design evokes a sense of decay and transformation, as if the keyboard itself were an ancient artifact unearthed from the future. Its weathered texture and muted tones are a nod to Arshamâ€™s signature "fictional archaeology" style, making it as much a statement piece as it is a functional tool. With hot swappable switches and a customizable layout, the Relic80 combines artistry with cutting-edge performance for a truly unique typing experience.
