About the Tofu65

The Tofu65 is the perfect entry point for those looking to dive into the world of custom mechanical keyboards without breaking the bank. Known for its affordability, it offers a solid aluminum case, a compact 65% layout, and endless customization options, making it a great choice for beginners. Despite its budget-friendly price, the Tofu65 delivers impressive build quality and a smooth typing experience, making it an excellent starting point for newcomers to the hobby.
